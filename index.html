About Me

I'm Harry Otieno Mokaya, a Software Engineer from Nairobi with roots in Migori County. I work across multiple domains from web and mobile development to machine learning and IoT systems. My focus is on building practical solutions that solve real problems.

Whether it's developing responsive web applications, training ML models, or creating IoT prototypes, I enjoy the challenge of turning ideas into working products. When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring fitness tech and wellness applications. I believe technology should be accessible and beneficial to everyone, which drives my approach to development.
